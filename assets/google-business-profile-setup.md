## Verification

Google will ask to verify. For service-area businesses this is usually **video verification**:
a short recording showing your work equipment (the mower/trailer), a flyer or business card
with the business name, and proof you operate in the area. Have the flyer and the mower handy
before starting. Verification review takes up to ~5 business days.
